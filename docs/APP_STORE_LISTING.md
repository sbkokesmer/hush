## Description (max 4000 chars)

Hush is a safer way to visit websites you don't fully trust.

Every site you add opens in a fully isolated session. The moment you close a tab, every cookie, every cache entry, every trace is wiped clean — as if you were never there.

WHAT HUSH PROTECTS YOU FROM
• Cross-site tracking — sites cannot see your activity on other sites
• Tracker scripts — Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, FullStory, Mixpanel, Segment, Sentry, and hundreds of other tracking and ad-network domains are blocked at the network layer
• Browser fingerprinting — Canvas, WebGL, Audio, Battery, and other fingerprinting APIs are masked
• Cookies — third-party cookies are blocked, first-party cookies are wiped on tab close
• Phishing and malware — Apple's fraudulent-website warning is enabled
• File downloads — blocked by default to prevent malware
• Clipboard reads — sites cannot snoop your clipboard
• WebRTC IP leaks — disabled
• Tracking parameters in URLs — utm_source, fbclid, gclid, msclkid and 40+ more are stripped automatically

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no signups, no email required
• No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporters
• No third-party SDKs that watch you
• Hush has no servers — there is nothing for anyone to log
• Reader Mode strips scripts and shows a clean, dark-themed view
• See exactly what site you're on: HTTPS status, certificate issuer, server IP

KEY FEATURES
• Add any site by URL, open it in fullscreen
• Site list ordered by most recently visited
• Privacy dashboard: trackers blocked, time saved, top sites
• Reader Mode for a clean, distraction-free read
• Site Info panel: certificate issuer, server IP, security status
• Onboarding to get you started in 30 seconds
• Beautiful, minimalist design

WHAT HUSH IS NOT
We believe in being honest. Hush is not a VPN — it does not hide your IP address. Hush is not an antivirus. Hush does not make you anonymous. If you log in to a website through Hush, that site knows who you are. What Hush does is reduce the amount of information sites and trackers can collect about you and your behavior.

NO COMPROMISE ON YOUR DATA
Hush keeps no logs. Hush sends nothing to a server. Everything stays on your device. The only network requests are the websites you choose to visit and a favicon for your site list.

If privacy matters to you, give Hush a try.

## App Privacy ("Data not collected")

When filling the App Privacy section in App Store Connect, choose:

> **No, we do not collect data from this app.**

Justification: Hush has no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs that observe the user, and stores all user data exclusively on-device using SwiftData. The only network requests made by the app are (a) website visits initiated by the user and (b) favicon fetches from a third-party (Google's favicon service), neither of which transmit identifying data to the developer.
