# Hush Support

## Quick help

**How do I add a site?**
On the Sites tab, tap the `+` button. Enter a name and a URL (e.g., `reddit.com`). You don't need to type `https://`.

**How do I open a site?**
Tap any row on the Sites tab. The site opens in a fullscreen, isolated session.

**How do I delete a site?**
Swipe left on the row in the Sites list and tap Delete.

**A site looks broken or won't load.**
Some websites use trackers as part of their core functionality and may misbehave when those scripts are blocked. You can:
- Pull down to refresh.
- Try toggling protections off in Settings to see whether a specific protection is causing the issue.
- Email us with the site name so we can look into a fix.

**Why is the tracker count zero on the Privacy tab?**
The first version of Hush stores cumulative counts per visit at the end of each session. Live in-tab counters will arrive in a future update.

**Does Hush hide my IP address?**
No. Hush is not a VPN. It blocks trackers, ads, fingerprinting, and cookies, but the websites you visit still see your real IP address. A VPN/Tor option is on the roadmap.

**Where is my data stored?**
Entirely on your device. Hush has no servers and sends nothing about you anywhere. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.

**How do I uninstall and remove all data?**
Press and hold the Hush icon on your home screen, tap "Remove App" → "Delete App". This removes all sites, settings, and statistics permanently.
